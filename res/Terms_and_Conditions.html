Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how the NaviCare pilot website collects, uses, and protects the personal information of its users. This website is being developed by student nurses of the West Visayas State University - College of Nursing, as partial fulfillment of their coursework. Rest assured, the student nurses are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the confidentiality of your information.

2. Information Collected

The website may collect limited user data for the purpose of evaluating its functionality and gathering feedback.

The types of information collected may include:

  • Login credentials
  • Usage data (e.g., pages visited, features used)
  • Feedback and survey responses

The student nurses do not collect sensitive medical records or private information through this website.

3. How Your Information is Used

The student nurses use the collected information to:

  • Evaluate the website's functionality and user experience.
  • Gather feedback for website improvement and maintenance.
  • Generate reports and analyses for academic purposes.

4. Data Protection and Security

The student nurses implement appropriate security measures to protect user data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

User data will be encrypted during transmission and storage.

Access to the Website and user data will be role-based, with tiered credentials for different user groups (e.g., students, faculty, patients).

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

The student nurses will not share or disclose your personal information to third parties except when required by law or with your explicit consent.

6. Data Retention

User data will be stored securely for one academic year. If you wish to sustain your account, a yearly update of credentials will be required, otherwise your account will be discontinued.

If your account has been discontinued, users are required to relog in through a new account to utilize the website's services.

Data will be disposed of after this period unless extended retention is approved through ethical clearance.

7. User Rights

Users may have the right to access, correct, or delete their personal information, depending on the Website's capabilities.

Users may also have the right to withdraw their consent for data collection, although this may limit their ability to use the Website.

8. Compliance

This Privacy Policy is in compliance with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 or Republic Act No. 10173 and the ethical standards of West Visayas State University - College of Nursing.

The WVSU - CON Institutional Review Board and the IT Compliance Team will oversee privacy and compliance throughout the project lifecycle.

9. Updates to this Privacy Policy

The student nurses may update this Privacy Policy without prior notice. Any changes will be posted on the website and users will be notified as appropriate for personal discretion.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the data practices, please contact juliedanica.lorena@wvsu.edu.ph.
